Hyundai is a Korean automaker that offers affordable cars and SUVs with a high level of content and a long warranty. Hyundai's first car was related to the Mini, and it has the world's biggest automobile manufacturing facility in South Korea. Hyundai also has a huge presence in America, where it produces some of its models. Hyundai has hired German talent to improve its design and performance, and has participated in various motorsport events. Hyundai's name means "modernity" and its badge represents two people shaking hands.
